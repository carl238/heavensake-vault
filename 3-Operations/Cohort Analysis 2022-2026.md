## Strategic Recommendations

1. **Protect the SGWS relationship** — Over 50% of US revenue flows through Southern Glazer's. This is both the largest opportunity and largest risk.
2. **Build the Large segment** — 35 customers at $10K–$50K represent the highest growth lever. Targeted cultivation could move them toward Whale status.
3. **Accelerate SKU expansion** — Prestige launched Dec 2024; continue new SKU introductions to drive incremental revenue per customer.
4. **Deepen CA / NY / FL** — The top 3 states represent $5.15M (56% of revenue). Deeper penetration here outweighs new state expansion.
5. **Address Jan–Feb trough** — Targeted promotions or incentive programs to smooth seasonality and support quarterly retention.
