## Notes

Carl is the ultimate decision-maker and brand philosopher at Heavensake. His leadership style combines deep strategic vision with a demanding execution standard. He frequently oscillates between Tokyo, Milan, Monaco, Ibiza, and New York. He is building toward a major marketing push for the 10th anniversary in June 2026 (Monaco GP) and a US commercial push in September 2026.

Key recurring themes:
- **Systems discipline** as the primary unlock: "The only thing standing between us and success are discipline in our systems."
- **Luxury authenticity** over mass-market tactics
- **Consumer pull** as the long-term goal: consumers walking into restaurants demanding Heavensake
- **80/20 focus**: top 20% of customers, top markets, top chef relationships
- Heavy influence from his personal network in Monaco, Ibiza, Tokyo, and the luxury/fashion world
