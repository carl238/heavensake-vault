## Organizational Principles

**Carl's standard for who stays on the team:**

> "One interesting test is if somebody were to call you and say, 'I am quitting. I have to.' How sad would you truly be that person quit? If your answer is 'I wouldn't really care. A part of me would actually be relieved' — you have a theoretically toxic negative person in your team." (Call 28)

**Taste as non-negotiable for brand roles:**

> "I don't think that Gloria has a great eye and great taste, so she cannot be a brand manager. I told her this." (Call 33)

**Team size philosophy:** HEAVENSAKE is being built to operate like the Anthropic marketing team — 1–3 people with AI tools doing the work of a 15-person agency.
