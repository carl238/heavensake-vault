## Organizational Principles

**Carl's standard for who stays on the team:**

> "One interesting test is if somebody were to call you and say, 'I am quitting. I have to.' How sad would you truly be that person quit? If your answer is 'I wouldn't really care. A part of me would actually be relieved' — you have a theoretically toxic negative person in your team."
> — *2026-02-09 Marketing Alignment*

**The profile Carl is building toward:**
- Hybrid individuals who combine AI tool fluency with genuine taste and cultural instincts
- Outcome-based compensation preferred (% of results, not hourly rates)
- People who push back when something is wrong — not yes-people

**Team size philosophy:** HEAVENSAKE is being built to operate like the Anthropic marketing team — 1–3 people with AI tools doing the work of a 15-person agency. Every hire should compound, not just add headcount.
