## Profile

Gloria is the first dedicated brand manager in HEAVENSAKE's history, joining on September 29, 2025. Her role is a hybrid between brand manager and project manager — Carl specifically describes it as "tailored for execution, organization, and brand sensitivity." She is based in the Milan office.

Carl brought her on because he wanted someone close to him who could extract ideas and bring order to the creative process: "Order in the madness."
