# Mio Okura

**Role:** Sales Manager — California / Japan / Asia
**Company:** Heavensake
**Relationship:** Manages California territory and Japan/Asia markets; handles high-end omakase accounts in CA; also part of Japan strategy
**Location:** Japan (primary) / California (territory)
**Contact:** mio.okura@heavensake.com

## Notes

Mio manages the California territory (LA, SF, San Diego, Orange County) and also handles the Japan market. She started in August 2024 and recently returned from maternity leave. Her territory is characterized by high-end omakase concentration but inconsistent market conditions — West LA is particularly volatile.

California market dynamics she has consistently reported:
- OC and San Diego more receptive than LA
- High-end accounts skeptical of premium price points (Prestige) but receptive to Noguchi
- Wellness/lifestyle angle has potential traction in CA
- Micro-influencer seeding recommended over mass influencer spend
