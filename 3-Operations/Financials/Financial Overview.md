## Sample & Chargeback Problem

Samples and chargebacks are a significant and poorly tracked cost center in the US market.

- Chargebacks include not just samples but also discounts beyond standard — this was a discovery in February 2026
- Zak Gross now approves all sample requests centrally (started March 2026)
- Goal: reduce sample/chargeback costs by minimum 20%
- Southern Glazers: chargeback process management being reviewed; Carl to pursue reclamation of outstanding funds

## Distribution Profitability

**The US distribution challenge:**
- Southern Glazers (national distributor): technical ordering system issues, high chargeback costs, limited sell-through in key markets
- The distributor model creates pricing compression — each tier takes margin
- Carl's diagnosis: HEAVENSAKE must **generate consumer demand directly** to pull through distributors, not rely on the distributor to create pull

**Market pruning:** Low-impact markets (Guatemala, Puerto Rico) being deprioritized. Resources concentrated in:
- Priority: US (Miami hero), Japan (e-commerce + brand), China/Hong Kong, Dubai
- Secondary: Orange County, San Diego, Santa Barbara, Palm Beach, Hamptons
