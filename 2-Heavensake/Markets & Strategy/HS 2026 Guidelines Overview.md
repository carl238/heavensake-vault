## The "Carl Filter" — Quick Reference

*(From the Brand Bible, Section IV)*

| NO | YES |
|---|---|
| "Tacky" marketing fluff (e.g., "where precision meets poetry") | **"Cool"** — Confident, minimal, culturally plugged-in |
| The word "Premium" — show, don't tell | **"Resonance"** — especially for Pink/Hanami line |
| "Old School" vibes — no wax seals, no dusty calligraphy | **Founders Content** — Carl or Etienne telling the real story |
| Generic wellness language | **Sensory truth** — how the liquid *feels* |
| Blending | **Assemblage** |
| Redefine | **Revive** |

## Hero Video Concepts (Sakura Prestige Launch)

- **Concept A (Artsy/Editorial):** Focus on the aesthetic "soul" of the bottle, using high-fashion and dreamlike transitions.
- **Concept B (Craftsmanship):** A deep dive into the physical creation process.
- **Alternative (ASMR Process):** A perfectly centered "still-frame" sequence. The bottle remains static while the treatment changes (sanded → kilned → hand-painted → bottled).

*Note: If the Hero Video is "Artsy," the Carousel must be "Craftsmanship" (and vice-versa) to ensure narrative balance.*
