## 2. ThruPlay = Passive Viewers (Confirmed by Data)

When ThruPlay is the optimization goal, Meta targets users most likely to **watch** 15+ seconds — not to like, comment, or share. These are fundamentally different behavioral signals served to different user segments.

**Our Butterfly data confirmed this:** 14,372 "engagements" → only 12 meaningful interactions (0.08%). 99.92% were passive 3-second auto-plays.

| Engagement Type | Likelihood in ThruPlay |
|----------------|----------------------|
| Video views (15+ sec) | **High** — algorithm-optimized |
| 3-second views | **Very high** — prerequisite |
| Link clicks | **Low–Moderate** — incidental |
| Likes/Reactions | **Low** — not algorithmically driven |
| Comments | **Very low** |
| Shares | **Very low** — rarest action |

**Rule:** Never judge ThruPlay success by social engagement. The value is building the viewership pool for retargeting.

## 3. ThruPlay Quality Diagnostics

Use these ratios to evaluate whether creative is working (per [Nate LC](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/nate-lc-7111a7132_how-to-analyze-ad-performance-using-metas-activity-7396244492762390528-eezN)):

| Metric | Strong | Needs Work |
|--------|--------|------------|
| ThruPlays ÷ 3-second plays | >20% | <10% |
| Average play time | >8 seconds | <3 seconds |
| High 3-sec + low ThruPlays | — | Hook works, content loses them |
| Both metrics weak | — | Rebuild creative from scratch |

**Cross-reference with conversion data:**
- Strong video metrics + weak CTR → CTA needs work
- Strong CTR + poor conversions → Landing page issue
- Everything performing → Scale

## 4. Audience Network Rewarded Video = Poison for Retargeting

[Jon Loomer (March 2025)](https://www.jonloomer.com/simplified-meta-ads-strategy/) documents that ThruPlay optimization inflates numbers through **Audience Network Rewarded Video** — third-party apps where users watch your video in exchange for virtual currency (game coins, extra lives). They have **zero brand interest**.

These viewers inflate ThruPlay counts but produce worthless retargeting audiences. You think you're building warm prospects; you're actually collecting gamers who wanted a free power-up.

**Rule:** Always check Ads Manager → Breakdown → By Placement. If Audience Network Rewarded Video consumes significant budget, exclude it. Our Rule #6 (manual placements: Facebook + Instagram only) should block this, but verify on every campaign.

## 5. Retargeting Audience Tiering Standard

Build these custom audiences from every ThruPlay campaign:

| Audience Segment | Watch Depth | Temperature | Retention Window |
|-----------------|-------------|-------------|-----------------|
| Casual viewers | 3-second views | Warm | 60–90 days |
| Interested viewers | 25–50% watched | Warmer | 60 days |
| High-intent viewers | 75–95% watched | Hot | 30 days |
| Completers | 95–100% watched | Hottest | 30 days |

**Critical insight:** Video viewer retargeting audiences convert **40% better** than standard website visitor retargeting, because Meta pre-qualifies them as engaged. Tailoring retargeting creatives to the specific video watched amplifies this further. ([Reddit r/FacebookAds, Feb 2026](https://www.reddit.com/r/FacebookAds/comments/1reonjd/i_was_about_to_give_up_on_retargeting_one_change/))

**Prerequisite:** Wait for at least **1,000 people per segment** before launching retargeting (typically 2–4 weeks depending on budget).

This builds on [[Meta Ads Performance Analysis — March 2026|Principle 5 from March 2026]]: 75% viewers > 15-second viewers for retargeting quality.

## 6. Retargeting Ad Types by Funnel Stage

| Funnel Stage | Audience | Ad Type | Content Strategy |
|-------------|----------|---------|-----------------|
| **MOFU** | 25–50% viewers | Social proof / UGC | Testimonials, press features, awards, "why HEAVENSAKE" storytelling |
| **MOFU** | 50–75% viewers | Educational carousel | Product range, food pairings, brewing process — address curiosity |
| **BOFU** | 75%+ viewers | Direct response / offer | Product showcase + clear CTA (shop now, limited edition, bundle) |
| **BOFU** | 95–100% viewers | Dynamic product ads | Specific products with urgency, free shipping, first-purchase discount |
| **Lookalike** | 1% based on 75%+ viewers | Best-performing HEAVENSAKE videos | Cold prospecting with proven creative |

**Key distinction:**
- **Reuse proven videos** → for Lookalike audiences (new cold prospects)
- **New tailored content** → for retargeting warm audiences (they've already seen the first video)

Source: [Stackmatix Funnel Strategy 2026](https://www.stackmatix.com/blog/meta-ads-funnel-strategy), [Deepsolv Full-Funnel Guide](https://deepsolv.ai/blog/how-to-build-a-full-funnel-strategy-with-meta-ads-step-by-step)

## 7. Budget Allocation Framework

| Funnel Stage | Budget Share | Purpose |
|-------------|-------------|---------|
| TOFU (ThruPlay campaigns) | 20–30% | Build viewership pool |
| MOFU (retargeting — trust) | 20–30% | Nurture warm audiences |
| BOFU (retargeting — conversion) | 40–50% | Drive purchases |

New brands (like HEAVENSAKE in the US) need more TOFU investment initially to build audiences. Shift toward BOFU as retargeting pools mature.

**Do not rush to BOFU without warming audiences first.** Cold audiences convert at much lower rates and higher CPAs. This aligns with our existing two-campaign sequence rule: engagement first, then conversion retargeting.
