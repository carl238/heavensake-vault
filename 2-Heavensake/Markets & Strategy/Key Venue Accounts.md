## Venue Account Philosophy

**HoReCa as "Brand Theatre":**
- Venues are not just sales channels — they are stages where the wine glass ritual is enforced, tasting notes are delivered, and guests experience the brand at its best
- Every venue partnership must create content that lives beyond the dining room
- Must funnel guests from the table to the DTC checkout: QR codes, business cards, event RSVPs

**Tier Assignment (see [[SOPs & Workflows]]):**
- Tier 1 accounts get full co-branded digital support (custom graphics, paid social, motion stories)
- Tier 3 accounts get story reposts only

**Revenue expectation:** Restaurants must achieve 70%+ margins for HEAVENSAKE to classify as a "reliable revenue generator" rather than a "premium risk."
