# Markets & Strategy

## Core Markets (Priority Order)

### 1. United States 🇺🇸
- **Status:** Primary market. Scale + cultural influence.
- **DTC:** Shopify store (heavensake.com / heavensake-us.myshopify.com)
- **Focus metros:** 6-10 high-value cities per campaign
- **Key channels:** Meta Ads, Klaviyo email, events, trade partnerships
- **Target:** Japanese-American communities, foodies, health-conscious, tech early adopters, affluent demographics

### 2. Japan 🇯🇵
- **Status:** Category birthplace + credibility market
- **Site:** jp.heavensake.com (launched Feb 2026)
- **Importance:** Legitimacy. A sake brand that Japan respects.

### 3. China 🇨🇳
- **Status:** Future growth engine (potential)
- **Timeline:** Post US/Japan establishment

---

## What Is NOT a Priority

- **Italy / European expansion** — [[Strategic Vetoes|Explicitly vetoed by Carl]]. Too small relative to effort.
- **Broad wholesale HoReCa dependency** — Goal is DTC-first, controlled channels

---

## Strategic Pillars

1. **DTC Engine** — High-conversion, data-capturing, drop-model capable
2. **Cultural Moments** — Art, gastronomy, fashion, nightlife — sake in every luxury context
3. **First-Party Data** — Email, phone, Club Heaven membership — own the relationship
4. **Drop Model** — Limited editions (Hanami, collabs, micro-lots) that sell out primarily online
