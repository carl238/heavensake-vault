## Key Strategic Learnings

### 1. "Maximize Engagement with a Post" Is a Vanity Metric Machine

When you optimize for post engagement on a video ad, Meta's algorithm finds the cheapest possible "engagement" — which is a 3-second auto-play. It is NOT finding people who like, comment, or share. The $0.01 CPE and "90% below peer median" flag from Meta are misleading — they reflect efficient delivery of a worthless metric.

**Rule going forward:** Never use "Maximize engagement with a post" for video content. It optimizes for auto-plays, not human attention or interaction.

### 2. ThruPlay Is the Correct Goal for Video Content

ThruPlay (15+ second views) cannot be gamed by auto-plays. Each ThruPlay represents a user who chose to watch. For HEAVENSAKE's cinematic content — whether AI-generated (butterfly) or real-world (Sake No Hana) — ThruPlay ensures the brand message is delivered.

**Setup path:** Engagement → On Your Ad → Video Views → ThruPlay Views

### 3. Creative Type Determines Strategy — Not Just Objective

| Creative Type | Example | Best Goal | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| AI-generated / bottle-as-hero | Butterfly reel | ThruPlay | Visual impact needs sustained viewing; no natural engagement trigger |
| Real-world / narrative / event | Sake No Hana x Seirinkan | ThruPlay | Story unfolds over time; brand message requires 15+ seconds |
| Quick-hit / trend / meme | (none yet) | Maximize Interactions | Immediate visual impact; engagement signals map to genuine reactions |

### 4. AI Bottle Content = Brand Wallpaper, Not Conversion Driver

The butterfly reel across 17,819 people reached generated: 1 link click, 0 profile visits, 2 saves, 6 shares. It has no conversion architecture — no product moment, no scarcity, no human desire trigger. AI bottle content builds visual brand recognition (top-of-funnel) but cannot drive action.

**Role in strategy:** Use for ThruPlay awareness campaigns to build the HEAVENSAKE visual universe. Do NOT use as conversion creative.

### 5. Real-World Content Has More Potential But Still Needs a Conversion Destination

The Sake No Hana reel has narrative depth, human characters, and sensory appeal — but the pop-up is over. There's nothing to book. Without a live conversion destination (e-commerce, event reservations, limited edition), no creative — however good — should be used for a conversion campaign.

**When to run conversion campaigns:** Only when there is a concrete, bookable/buyable action. Examples:
- Product available for purchase online (e-commerce live in target market)
- Event with open reservations (future Sake No Hana dinner, Monaco 10th anniversary)
- Limited edition drop (Prestige 3, Hanami bottle)
- Retail activation (Erewhon-style geotargeting)

### 6. Manus AI Recommendations — Partially Correct

| Manus Claim | Assessment |
|---|---|
| ThruPlay is the optimal goal | **Correct** — validated by data |
| Meta replaced Post Engagement with Maximize Interactions | **Partially correct** — Maximize Interactions is smarter (multi-signal AI), but still not ideal for video. See TrackBee analysis. |
| Maximize Interactions "only optimizes for likes, comments, shares, saves" | **Incorrect** — it also considers video views and link clicks, weighted by predicted downstream conversion value |
| Disable Advantage+ Audience | **Correct and critical** — alcohol compliance requirement |
| 8-city targeting | **Overly broad for $300** — recommend 3-4 cities max to achieve meaningful frequency |

## Decisions Made (March 30, 2026)

| Decision | Rationale |
|---|---|
| **Butterfly conversion campaign (Website Leads) DISCARDED** | Butterfly creative has no conversion architecture. 1 link click across 17,819 reach. Would starve conversion algorithm of learning data. Draft deleted from Ads Manager. |
| **Sake No Hana boost: use ThruPlay** | 30-second cinematic narrative requires sustained attention. ThruPlay finds attentive viewers. |
| **No conversion campaign until real conversion destination exists** | Neither butterfly nor Sake No Hana has a bookable/buyable CTA. Stay in awareness mode. |
| **AI bottle content repositioned as brand wallpaper** | Good for visual universe building (ThruPlay). Not for conversion. Real-world content with human stories drives intent. |
| **Placement restriction: Reels + Stories only for 9:16 content** | Butterfly campaign ran across Feed, Explore, Threads, Notifications — most display vertical content poorly. |
