# Meta Ads — Strategic Principles (March 2026)

Principles extracted from the Label Noir Butterfly campaign analysis (March 30, 2026). These are durable rules, not campaign-specific.

---

## 1. Never Use "Maximize Engagement with a Post" for Video

Meta's algorithm counts 3-second auto-plays as "engagements." It finds serial auto-players, not people who think, react, or act. The butterfly campaign proved this: 14,372 "engagements" contained only 12 meaningful human interactions (0.08%). The $0.01 CPE was a vanity metric.

**Rule:** All video campaigns must use ThruPlay (15+ second views) as the performance goal. Path: Engagement → On Your Ad → Video Views → ThruPlay Views.

---

## 2. Creative Type Defines the Role, Not Just the Objective

| Creative Type | Strategic Role | What It Can Do | What It Cannot Do |
|---|---|---|---|
| AI-generated / bottle-as-hero | Brand wallpaper | Build visual universe, ThruPlay awareness | Drive conversion — no desire/scarcity trigger |
| Real-world / event / lifestyle | Brand storytelling | Build warm audiences, create intent | Convert without a live bookable/buyable action |
| Product-focused with CTA | Conversion driver | Drive traffic/sales with live e-commerce | Work as top-of-funnel (too commercial) |
| Limited edition / scarcity | Conversion trigger | Create urgency and action | Work as general awareness (wastes scarcity) |

AI content builds the aesthetic universe. Real-world content gives people a reason to enter it. Neither converts without a concrete destination.

---

## 3. No Conversion Campaign Without a Live Destination

A conversion campaign requires a concrete action the viewer can take right now. Without one, the algorithm starves during its learning phase (needs ~50 events/week) and you burn budget educating a system that can't learn.

**Valid conversion destinations:** e-commerce purchase, event reservation, limited edition drop, retail activation (geotargeted).

**Invalid:** brand awareness content with no CTA destination, past events, general brand building.

---

## 4. 9:16 Video Belongs in Reels + Stories Only

Vertical content displayed in Feed, Explore, Threads, Notifications, and Search Results loses its production value. Manual placements: IG Reels, IG Stories, FB Reels, FB Stories. Exclude everything else for 9:16 cinematic content.

---

## 5. Manus AI: Trust But Verify

Manus was correct on ThruPlay and Advantage+ compliance. Manus was wrong on how Maximize Interactions works (it does consider video views and link clicks, not just reactions). Manus suggested 8 cities for $300 — too broad. Always validate AI vendor recommendations against real performance data.

---

## 6. Budget Concentration > Geographic Spread

$300 across 8 metros = noise. $300 across 3-4 metros = meaningful frequency (1.5-2.0). For small budgets, fewer cities with deeper penetration beats wider geographic coverage.

## Decisions Log (March 30, 2026)

| Decision | Rationale |
|---|---|
| Butterfly conversion campaign discarded | No conversion architecture in the creative |
| ThruPlay mandatory for all video | Post engagement optimization delivers auto-plays |
| AI bottle content = awareness only | Beautiful but no intent trigger |
| Conversion campaigns deferred | No live buyable/bookable destination exists |
| Reels + Stories only for vertical video | Other placements degrade production value |
