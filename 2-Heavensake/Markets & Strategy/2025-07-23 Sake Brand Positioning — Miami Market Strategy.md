# Sake Brand Positioning — Miami Market Strategy — 2025-07-23

## Summary
The meeting highlighted the strategic positioning of sake as an everyday beverage, akin to white wine, to broaden its appeal beyond sushi restaurants. Miami was identified as the primary market due to its cultural alignment and nightlife connections, prioritizing local consumer education through social media and web platforms. The target demographic consists of health-conscious individuals aged 30-50 seeking refined alternatives to hard alcohol. A three-year goal was set to establish the leading sake brand in Miami, emphasizing the need for a robust sales force and direct-to-consumer strategies for effective market penetration. The discussion also referenced the Olympia Vodka case study to underscore the value of chef collaborations and influencer partnerships while noting the importance of supply chain optimization before ramping up marketing efforts.
