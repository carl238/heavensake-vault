# SAKURA PRESTIGE Meta Ads Strategic Plan

*Source: Sakura-Prestige-Meta-Ads-Strategic-Plan-19.pdf*

**US Launch Campaign | March 13–29, 2026**
**Prepared for:** HEAVENSAKE
**Campaign:** Sakura Prestige Limited Edition Launch
**Budget:** $2,000–$2,500 USD
**Lead Target:** 1,000–1,500 high-quality leads
**Campaign Window:** March 13–29, 2026
**Inventory:** 30 units VIP tier | 30 units Club Heaven tier

---

## Executive Summary

This strategic plan outlines a Meta Ads campaign designed to generate 1,000–1,500 high-quality leads for the Sakura Prestige launch in the US market within a $2,000–$2,500 budget. The campaign leverages scarcity positioning (only 60 units total), tiered access strategy, and HEAVENSAKE's luxury brand DNA to drive prestige demand while maintaining efficient acquisition costs.

**Key Strategic Pillars:**
- **Lead-Gen-First Approach:** Meta Lead Ads (Instant Forms) to minimize friction and maximize volume within budget constraints
- **Tiered Funnel Architecture:** TOFU awareness → MOFU qualification → BOFU conversion, aligned with VIP/Club Heaven/Database engagement timeline
- **Brand Voice Integrity:** All creative adheres to HEAVENSAKE's "luxury understatement" principles—no generic premium language, emphasis on Assemblage craft and cultural narrative
- **Technical Foundation:** Pixel + CAPI implementation with Standard Events and Klaviyo integration for seamless lead nurturing starting March 17

**Expected Outcomes:**
- 1,200–1,500 qualified leads (base case)
- $18–$28 CPL average (US luxury market benchmark)
- 8–12% Lead Form conversion rate
- Clean handoff to Klaviyo for March 17/21 engagement sequences

## 1. Audience Strategy

### 1.1 Core US Targeting Framework

Based on [[Buyer Personas]] and US luxury beverage market dynamics, the campaign targets three primary audience segments:

**Primary Personas**

**The Social Host (45–60, Female, $200k+, Premium Markets)**
- Targeting: Architectural Digest, Net-a-Porter, luxury home/entertaining interests
- Pain Point: "Bored of serving the same Chardonnay—I want to impress guests"
- Message Angle: Object of desire, stunning packaging, conversation starter
- Priority: HIGH (Core Growth driver)

**The Gastro Adventurer (30–45, Male, $150k+, NYC/Miami/SF/LA)**
- Targeting: Michelin Guide, fine dining, omakase, mixology, Bon Appétit
- Pain Point: "Standard wine falls flat with fusion/umami flavors"
- Message Angle: Perfect pairing for complex cuisines, umami bomb
- Priority: HIGH (Core Growth driver)

**The Champagne Switcher (24–35, Mix M/F, $100k+, Urban/Nightlife Centers)**
- Targeting: Soho House, Hypebeast, music festivals, luxury nightlife
- Pain Point: "Champagne makes me bloated—I want energy for the night"
- Message Angle: "A Better High"—euphoric buzz, no bloating, clean celebration
- Priority: MEDIUM (Visibility lever, volume driver)

**Secondary Personas (Lower Priority, Niche Segments)**

*The Sulfite Hater (35–50, Female, Wellness-Focused)*
- Health-conscious wine alternatives, sulfite-free positioning
- Targeting: Goop, Erewhon, yoga/Pilates, clean eating

*The Japanophile/Craft Explorer (28–50, Cultural Connoisseurs)*
- Omakase dining, Japanese design, craft culture
- Messaging: Technical mastery, Assemblage innovation

### 1.2 Audience Build Strategy

**Cold Prospecting (TOFU)**
- Interest-Based Targeting:
  - Luxury dining & fine wine (Michelin restaurants, wine enthusiasts, sommelier culture)
  - Design & entertaining (Architectural Digest, luxury home, event hosting)
  - Fashion & nightlife (Soho House, luxury lifestyle, music festivals, premium spirits)
  - Wellness luxury (organic lifestyle, sulfite-free, clean eating + luxury intersection)
- Geographic Focus: US Tier-1 luxury markets
  - Primary: NYC, LA, SF, Miami, Chicago, Boston, Seattle, Austin
  - Secondary: Hamptons, Aspen, Napa, Charleston (seasonal/affluent zones)
- Demographics: Age 28–60, Income: Top 10–25% household income zip codes, Education: College degree+

**Lookalike Audiences (MOFU Entry Point)**
1. 1% LAL – Top 20% Customers (Whales) — Highest-intent prospecting
2. 2–3% LAL – Engaged Email List — Broader qualified prospecting
3. 3–5% LAL – Website Visitors (90-day) — Scale audience once core segments saturate

**Retargeting & Custom Audiences (MOFU/BOFU)**
- Warm Traffic (MOFU): 30-day site visitors, 14-day video viewers (75%+), 7-day Lead Ad openers
- Hot Retargeting (BOFU): Landing page visitors (7-day), Add-to-cart / Initiate Checkout (7-day)
- Suppression Lists: Existing customers (180 days), VIP/Club Heaven members, Lead form submitters (after 48 hours)

## 4. Creative & Messaging Strategy

### 4.1 Brand Voice Guardrails (Non-Negotiable)

All creative must adhere to HEAVENSAKE's "luxury understatement" principles.

**Banned Words/Phrases:**
- "Premium" (show luxury through craft, never label it)
- "Blend/Blending" (we practice Assemblage, not industrial blending)
- "Where precision meets poetry" (clichéd agency-speak)
- Generic wellness language ("clean," "natural" without sensory context)
- Japanese clichés without Franco-Japanese lens (Mount Fuji wallpaper, cherry blossoms for decoration only)

**Required Language/Concepts:**
- Assemblage (not blending): Artistic elevation, third color metaphor
- Pure Elevation (public face): Light, purity, social status, spiritual lift
- A Better High (visceral engine): Cleaner buzz, no sugar crash, euphoric without heaviness
- The Ritual: Wine glass (never ochoko), floral nose, silky mouthfeel
- Founders' Narrative: Régis Camus (8x IWC Winemaker of the Year), meeting of Toji mastery + French joie de vivre

**Visual Style:**
- Stanley Kubrick meets Daft Punk: High contrast, minimalism, white space, neon accents
- Studio-grade photography only (no grainy images)
- Torii gate and hexagon symbols
- Bottle as "brush full of ink flowing upward"

### 4.2 Ad Angles for Prestige Positioning + Scarcity

**Angle 1: Scarcity + Exclusivity (Primary Driver)**
- Headline: "Only 30 Bottles. Secure Your Allocation."
- Why it works: Authentic scarcity, appeals to Top 20 Whales and Social Host

**Angle 2: Assemblage Craft (Heritage + Innovation)**
- Headline: "When French Artistry Meets Japanese Mastery"
- Why it works: Appeals to Gastro Adventurers and Japanophiles

**Angle 3: The Ritual (Wine Glass, Pure Elevation)**
- Headline: "Pour. Breathe. Elevate."
- Why it works: Appeals to Social Hosts (ritual + entertaining), differentiates from ochoko

**Angle 4: A Better High (Visceral, Nightlife, Celebration)**
- Headline: "The Celebration Without the Crash"
- Why it works: Appeals to Champagne Switchers, addresses wellness concern without generic health-language

### 4.3 Creative Asset Specifications

**Image Ads:** 1:1 (square) primary, 4:5 (vertical) secondary | Min 1080x1080

**Video Ads:** 15–30 seconds | 1:1 or 9:16 | Hook in first 3 seconds | Burned-in subtitles
- Storyboard Example (30 sec): 0–3 sec: Bottle close-up, "30" overlaid | 3–10 sec: Slow-motion pour | 10–18 sec: Friends toasting | 18–25 sec: "Only 30 Bottles" text | 25–30 sec: HEAVENSAKE logo + CTA

**Carousel Ads (3–5 cards):**
1. "Only 30 Bottles Exist"
2. "Assemblage by Régis Camus + Master Toji"
3. "Designed for the Wine Glass"
4. "Pure Elevation—No Crash, No Bloat"
5. "Reserve Your Access Now"

### 4.4 Compliance Checklist
- [ ] No minors in imagery
- [ ] No excessive consumption implied
- [ ] No health/medical benefit claims
- [ ] Heritage/craft/lifestyle messaging prioritized
- [ ] 21+ targeting confirmed in all ad sets

## 6. Technical Setup & Tracking

### 6.1 Meta Pixel + Conversions API (CAPI) Implementation

**Required Standard Events:**
- PageView (all pages)
- ViewContent (Sakura Prestige LP, product pages)
- Lead (form submissions, newsletter signups)
- AddToCart
- InitiateCheckout
- Purchase (with value and currency parameters)

**Deduplication:** Use `event_id` parameter to prevent double-counting when both Pixel + CAPI fire for same event.

### 6.2 Klaviyo Integration

**Lead Ad Form Field Mapping:**
| Meta Lead Ad Field | Klaviyo Property |
|---|---|
| Email | Email (profile ID) |
| First Name | First Name |
| Last Name | Last Name |
| Phone | Phone Number |
| Custom: Tier Interest | sakura_tier_interest (VIP/Club/Waitlist) |
| Custom: Excitement Reason | sakura_excitement |

**Engagement Flows:**
- Flow 1 (March 17): "Club Heaven Early Access" email to synced Club Heaven leads
- Flow 2 (March 21): "Sakura Prestige Launch" email to all leads + existing database

### 6.3 Lead Form Questions

1. Email (required, auto-filled)
2. First Name (required, auto-filled)
3. Last Name (optional, auto-filled)
4. Phone (optional)
5. Custom Q1: "Which Sakura Prestige tier interests you?" (VIP Early Access / Club Heaven Access / General Waitlist)
6. Custom Q2: "What excites you most about Sakura Prestige?" (multi-select: Limited edition, Japanese craft + French artistry, Exclusive access, Perfect for special occasions)

**Post-Submit Experience:**
- Thank-you screen: "You're on the list. Check your email on [March 17/21] for exclusive access."
- Instant email confirmation via Klaviyo (synced within 5 minutes)
