## Brand Philosophy & Strategy Terms

**Assemblage** — The defining artisanal technique of HEAVENSAKE. Borrowed from high-end French Champagne-making and introduced to sake by cellar master Régis Camus, it is the artistic layering of different sakes to create a complex, harmonious "third color" (like mixing blue and yellow to create purple). See [[Brand Story — The Assemblage]] for the full narrative.

**Blending** — The traditional, industrial counterpart to Assemblage. In HEAVENSAKE's vocabulary, "blending" is rejected as a term, as it implies mixing batches purely for taste uniformity, volume management, or cost-saving. See [[Never Say List]] → "Blend / Blending."

**Reviving (Not Redefining)** — The core mission of the brand. HEAVENSAKE expressly forbids the use of the marketing cliché "redefining sake." Instead, the goal is to *revive* a dormant UNESCO cultural heritage. See [[Brand Voice Bible]] → Section I.

**Pure Elevation** — The elegant, public-facing value proposition of the brand. Represents the physical purity of the liquid and the spiritual and social lift experienced when drinking it.

**A Better High** — The visceral, lifestyle-driven "internal engine" of the brand. Used primarily for nightlife and celebration contexts. See [[Narrative Pillars]] → Pillar 8.

**Meeting of Masters** — The foundational concept of Franco-Japanese cultural exchange between Régis Camus and the Japanese Tojis. The mythological heart of the brand story.

**The 15-Year Drop** — The pristine water used to brew the sake takes 15 years to filter through the Japanese mountains. *"Eternity in a drop."* A core storytelling asset. See [[The Lexicon]] → Heritage, Craft & Nature.

**Assemblage of Moments** — The thematic concept for HEAVENSAKE's 10-year anniversary, celebrating how the brand has brought sake into versatile, modern luxury settings — from fashion shows and art galleries to Michelin-starred dining and nightclub takeovers. See [[10th Anniversary — Assemblage of Moments]].

## The Ritual & Terminology

**The Wine Glass Ritual** — HEAVENSAKE's non-negotiable rule for consumption. Sake must be served chilled in a large wine glass to allow the floral nose and silky mouthfeel to fully express. See [[Brand Voice Bible]] → Section III (The Non-Negotiable Pillars).

**Ochoko** 猪口 — The traditional small ceramic sake cup. HEAVENSAKE explicitly rejects the *ochoko* because its shape removes 30–40% of the aromatic experience. Small cups are also functionally "annoying" (constant refilling) and culturally limit the drink to a sushi counter.

**Junmai** 純米 — Pure rice sake with no additives, no added sugar, gluten-free, and sulfite-free. HEAVENSAKE exclusively produces Junmai sake. *"Nothing added. Nothing taken away."*

**Toji** 杜氏 — A traditional Japanese sake brewmaster. *"The soul of the kura."* The highest title in the brewing hierarchy.

## Symbols & Aesthetics

**The Torii Gate** — The core graphic element of HEAVENSAKE's visual identity. Represents the passage from the mundane world into the "Heaven" of the brand experience. Used in merchandising and 10-year anniversary assets.

**The Daruma** — Traditional Japanese doll symbolizing perseverance. Used for the 10-year anniversary ritual, where the act of painting the doll's eyes represents celebrating past achievements and looking toward a new chapter.

**The Hexagon** — Represents the structured French heritage (*L'Hexagone*) meeting Japanese artistry and the organic nature of Japan.

**Pink Resonance** — The creative concept driving the *Hanami / Sakura Prestige* launch — symbolizing spring, rebirth, and fleeting elegance. See [[Core Keywords]] → Resonance.

## Digital & Community

**Club Heaven** — The brand's inner-circle community and VIP membership tier. Grants the top 20% of high-net-worth clients access to exclusive allocations, concierge services, and global luxury events. The primary channel for the [[Buyer Personas]] → VHNW Collector persona.

**GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)** — The digital strategy to structure content so that AI engines (Gemini, ChatGPT, Claude) recognize the brand as an authority in luxury sake. A 2026 strategic priority.

**The Content Machine** — The 2026 strategic shift toward high-volume, AI-assisted, yet brand-aligned digital output to maintain a "magazine-vibe" rhythm across social channels.
