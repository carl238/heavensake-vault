## The Assemblage Principle

Every Heavensake product is composed through **Assemblage** by cellar master **[[Régis Camus]]** (Chef-de-cave at Maison Rare Champagne / Piper-Heidsieck, 8-time IWC "Sparkling Winemaker of the Year"). This is the brand's technical signature — applying the French champagne blending philosophy to Japanese sake. It's not just production; it's authorship.

## Strategy

The portfolio serves two distinct audiences:
- **$35–$75 range** → Food and culture enthusiasts. Accessible luxury. Volume.
- **$700+ range** → Ultra-premium collectors. Statement pieces. Margin and prestige.

The "drop model" (limited editions like Sakura, anniversary bottles, collabs) bridges both — creates urgency, drives DTC, and generates cultural moments.

Prestige I production is hard-capped at ~500 bottles/year by [[Noguchi Naohiko Sake Institute]]. This constraint is a feature, not a bug.
