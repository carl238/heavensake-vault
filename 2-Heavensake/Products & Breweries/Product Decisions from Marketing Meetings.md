## Sake Baby — Phase-Out Story

**Status:** Globally discontinued as of late 2025

| Date | Decision / Update | Who |
|------|-------------------|-----|
| **2025-11-03** | **Global phase-out confirmed.** Europe already removed. Now being discontinued globally. Carafe proposed as on-premise replacement (matching 10oz format). Carl skeptical of bottle-shaped ceramic carafes: *"Imagine a small Dom Perignon in ceramic, just lame."* | Zak Gross |
| **2025-11-03** | Action: evaluate carafe inventory, cost, design viability as replacement. If design satisfactory and price right, produce more. If not, source better design via new Japanese porcelain connections. | Zak Gross |
| **2025-07-21** | **Phase-out first confirmed.** Sake Baby 300ml being phased out of portfolio. Accounts relying on it (e.g., Lobster Club's 300ml offering) to find alternatives. | Nes Rueda / John Pak |

**Strategic rationale:** Sake Baby was a lower-price-point SKU inconsistent with the brand's luxury repositioning. The carafe (10oz, comparable format) replaces the on-premise use case while elevating the brand's visual and cultural presentation.
