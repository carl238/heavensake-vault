## Overview

The 13 narrative pillars define the story territory HEAVENSAKE owns across all content, PR, and marketing. Each pillar is a thematic area that content, copy, and campaigns should activate. They are not campaign briefs — they are the underlying belief system that makes every piece of copy feel coherent and intentional.

## The 13 Pillars

### 1. The Art of Assemblage
**Type:** Narrative Pillar
**Notion URL:** https://www.notion.so/32959406dc5881f88a1fe461a81ca4ea

**Short Version:** French cellar master technique meets Japanese brewing mastery. Not fusion — dialogue.

**Do / Don't:**
- DO: Say *assemblage*, *dialogue*, *conversation*
- DON'T: Say *blend*, *fusion*, *mix*

**Full Content:** See [[Brand Story — The Assemblage]] for the complete narrative, including the "third color" metaphor, Régis Camus's role, and the DO/DON'T framework.

## How to Use These Pillars

Each content piece, campaign, or collaboration should map to at least one — ideally two — of these pillars. The pillars are not mutually exclusive; most strong content will activate multiple pillars simultaneously.

**Example:** A tasting video featuring Régis Camus at the Dassai brewery activates:
- Pillar 1: The Art of Assemblage
- Pillar 5: East-West Alchemy
- Pillar 12: Craft Without Compromise
- Pillar 11: The Drink of the Gods, Elevated

If a piece of content cannot map to any pillar — it should not exist.
