## 📋 Quick Reference

### Approved Seed Phrases (Sensory Hooks)
- *Silk on the tongue.*
- *A garden in bloom on the nose.*
- *Whisper of pear. Breath of jasmine.*
- *The sound of water meeting glass.*
- *First sip — a quiet rebellion.*
- *Moonlight in liquid form.*
- *The scent of spring through open shoji doors.*
- *Born in Japan, refined in France.*
- *20 centuries of brewing, reimagined.*
- *Eternity in a drop.*
- *An Assemblage of Moments.*

### Signature Brand Lines
- *HEAVENSAKE — The Art of Franco-Japanese Sake.*
- *Pure by nature, elevated by art.*
- *From Japan's finest breweries, blended by France's greatest cellar master.*

### The Ultimate Copy Filter
Before publishing anything:
1. Is this generic?
2. Could a standard agency have written this in an afternoon?

If yes to either — delete it. Start over.
