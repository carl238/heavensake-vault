## Video Content Strategy

### 1. The Hero Video (Launch Day)

- **Concept A (Artsy/Editorial):** Focus on the aesthetic "soul" of the bottle, using high-fashion and dreamlike transitions.
- **Concept B (Craftsmanship):** A deep dive into the physical creation.
- **Alternative Idea (ASMR Process):** A perfectly centered "still-frame" sequence. The bottle remains static while the treatment changes (sanded → kilned → hand-painted → bottled).

### 2. Teaser & Post-Launch Support

- **Teaser (March 13):** A 5–10 second high-impact AI-generated sequence to build "Pink Resonance" hype.
- **Post-Launch Carousel:** An educational deep dive into the "Work & Dedication" behind the bottle.
  - *Note:* If the Hero Video is "Artsy," the Carousel must be "Craftsmanship" (and vice-versa) to ensure narrative balance.
