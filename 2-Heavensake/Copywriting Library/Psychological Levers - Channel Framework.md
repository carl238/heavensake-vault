# Psychological Levers — Channel Copywriting Framework

*Comprehensive psychological lever framework mapped to each HEAVENSAKE channel. Match the right emotional trigger to the channel's native context — do not apply levers uniformly.*

## The Master Lever Map

| Channel | Primary Lever | Secondary Lever | Trigger Mechanism |
|---------|--------------|----------------|-------------------|
| **Instagram Feed/Reels** | Desire | Heritage | Sensory-first visuals; "the vibe before the fact" |
| **Email — Welcome Flow** | Heritage | Ritual | Origin story arc across 4 emails; assemblage education |
| **Email — Segmented Drops** | Scarcity | Exclusivity | Limited edition waitlist language; allocation priority |
| **TikTok / Reels (Champagne Switcher)** | Desire | Scarcity | Energy narrative, party-coded short-form, FOMO-driven pacing |
| **YouTube / Long-Form** | Heritage | Ritual | Craft deep-dives, Régis Camus POV, assemblage process |
| **Product Page / Site** | Desire | Heritage | Lead with desire, support with facts; never open on price |
| **Direct / Private Client** | Exclusivity | Scarcity | Allocation language, concierge access, "liquid art" framing |
| **Gifting / Packaging Copy** | Desire | Ritual | Short, iconic, emotionally charged; "object of desire" framing |
| **Events / Partnerships** | Ritual | Heritage | Immersive tasting, cultural ceremony, sensory experience |
| **PR / Press Kit** | Heritage | Exclusivity | Cinematic myth-making; the Meeting of Masters narrative |

---

## DESIRE

The highest-frequency lever — it belongs on every channel but must be grounded in sensory experience, never generic aspiration. The copy guidelines are explicit: *evocative* beats *descriptive*.

- **Instagram:** Desire is triggered by a visual hook before any brand truth.
- **Product Page:** The formula is "lead with desire, support with facts."
- **Social Host persona (45–60, Hamptons):** The desire trigger is object-level — the bottle as conversation starter, something guests will ask about.

---

## SCARCITY

Scarcity increases perceived value only when it feels true, not manufactured. The brand's own strategy frames this as: introduce limited edition releases or seasonal specials, making certain products available only at specific times or in limited quantities.

- **Email:** Waitlist language for the VHNW Collector and Craft Connoisseur ("allocation priority, concierge updates"), not countdown timers.
- **TikTok/Reels:** Scarcity is implied through energy and cultural heat — the sense that this moment is passing — rather than explicit "only X left" copy.

---

## RITUAL

Ritual is the most underleveraged lever in the channel mix. The strategy documents flag *symbolism and ritual* as a key luxury brand tool — the hexagonal logo and assemblage concept should function as "symbols of Franco-Japanese heritage," not just design elements.

- **Email:** Ritual is built through the educational welcome flow: a 4-email sequence that teaches temperature, glassware, and occasion before asking for a purchase.
- **Events:** The tasting itself *is* the ritual — the sequence of presentation, the pour, the nose — and copy should frame the serving experience as ceremony, not instruction.

---

## HERITAGE

Heritage is the most credible authority signal HEAVENSAKE has — and the one most distinctly its own. The "Meeting of Masters" narrative (Régis Camus, 8× Best Champagne Winemaker, meets Dassai, Tatenokawa, Urakasumi) is the central myth.

- **YouTube and long-form content:** Heritage is deployed as the *primary* lever — Régis Camus and the Japanese Tojis are treated as characters, not credentials.
- **Craft Connoisseur and Japanophile personas:** Heritage communicates "cultural authority," not nostalgia — readable luxury that respects tradition while delivering modern expression.

---

## EXCLUSIVITY

Exclusivity must be structural, not just verbal — the strategy is explicit that the brand should never discount in copy, because "value is communicated through exclusivity, craft, and experience, never price reduction."

- **VHNW Collector:** Exclusivity is activated through *access* mechanics: direct outreach, private events, allocation lists, investment/provenance narrative.
- **Instagram:** Exclusivity is implied by placement (select high-end venues, Michelin-starred chef endorsements) and by what is *not* shown — mass-market contexts, price callouts, or promotional framing.
