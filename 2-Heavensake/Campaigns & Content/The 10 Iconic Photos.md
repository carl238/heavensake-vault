# The 10 Iconic Photos

Carl's personally selected images for the 10th anniversary campaign. These are the defining visual moments of HEAVENSAKE's first decade. Not the pictures themselves — **Carl's narrated explanation of each photo is what makes the campaign powerful.** (Call 26)

> "Not the picture itself but Carl's narrated explanation of each of the 10 iconic photos is what makes it powerful." (Call 26)

The campaign approach: Carl speaks (without necessarily showing his face) over each image, providing the context, the feeling, and the cultural significance of the moment. This narration transforms documentation into storytelling.

## Campaign Direction

**Format:** Carousel or video series — each photo gets its own frame, its own explanation.

**Carl's narration style:**
- Without necessarily showing his face
- Personal, intimate, honest
- The WHY of each moment, not just the WHAT
- Carl is "hyping himself tastefully" — owning the brand's achievements without boasting

> "Finding ways to hype yourself in a way that is elegant and cool and not like — it's actually not that easy and there's not that many opportunities. So this is a very, very good opportunity to put in perspective what it is that we're doing, why we're doing it and why it matters." (Call 16)

**Timing:** Major campaign starting April/May 2026. Anchors the 10th anniversary narrative through the Monaco party in June and the US September activation.

**Production:** Filmed by [[Urai]] (trusted videographer). (Call 26)

## The Shibari Photo — Historical Context

At the Paris launch in 2016, HEAVENSAKE hosted shibari performances — indicating the brand's edgier, more underground origins that have since matured into a luxury aesthetic. This is part of the brand's authentic history. (Call 8)

Including this in the 10 photos is deliberate: it shows the brand's full arc from underground cultural moment to global luxury brand.

---

## The RZA Moment — Why It Matters

> "RZA is forever the first rapper to have brought a bottle of sake on stage." (Call 16)

> "We're literally the first sake brand ever to go and have access to such musicians. And they bring sake on stage and they bring sake onto their podcasts." (Call 16)

This is a historic first — not just for HEAVENSAKE, but for the entire sake category. HEAVENSAKE pioneered sake in music/celebration contexts. This deserves to be hyped, tastefully.

## Caption Strategy for This Campaign

**Two-type reader optimization:**
- **Skimmers:** Each photo needs a one-line hook that stops the scroll
- **Readers:** The full caption provides Carl's narration — the context, the feeling, the moment's significance

**SEO-optimize:** Each caption should be optimized for the subject's search terms (RZA + sake, Vogue + sake, etc.)

**Tag everyone:** Tag the people in each photo, the venues, the collaborators. (Call 10)
