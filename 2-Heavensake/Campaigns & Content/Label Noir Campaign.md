# Label Noir Butterfly Campaign — Decisions & Lessons

> **Product:** Label Noir — Junmai Daiginjo (~$92)
> **Creative:** Label Noir Butterfly reel (AI-generated, bottle-as-hero)

---

## Key Decisions

| Date | Decision | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| March 21 | Advantage+ audience → campaign rejected | Meta overrode 21+ age-gating. Audience ballooned from ~1M to 200M+. |
| March 24 | Switched to Original Audience permanently | Alcohol compliance. Advantage+ treats age as suggestion, not hard limit. |
| March 24 | National U.S. → 8 metro targeting | $300 budget too thin for national. Focus on markets with Japanese communities, foodies, health-conscious consumers. |
| March 24 | Removed income filter for awareness | Portfolio starts at $35. Income gating blocks potential entry-level buyers. |
| March 24 | Added champagne interests to targeting | Régis Camus storytelling is central. Dom Pérignon, Krug, PIPER-HEIDSIECK reach the right consumer. |
| March 30 | **Conversion campaign (Website Leads) discarded** | Butterfly creative has no conversion architecture. 1 link click across 17,819 reach. AI bottle content is brand wallpaper, not a conversion driver. |
| March 30 | **AI bottle content role defined** | Awareness/ThruPlay only. Does not drive action, desire, or scarcity. |

---

## Lessons Learned

1. **Never trust Advantage+ for alcohol.** It will override age restrictions. Always Original Audience with 21+ hard limit.
2. **"Post Engagement" optimization on video = auto-play farming.** 99.92% of "engagements" were 3-second auto-plays. Use ThruPlay for all video.
3. **AI-generated content is brand wallpaper.** Visually stunning but no intent trigger. Cannot drive clicks, saves, shares, or conversions at meaningful rates.
4. **Don't force a conversion campaign without a conversion destination.** If there's nothing to buy or book, stay in awareness mode.
5. **Meta's "90% below peer median" badge is misleading.** It means you're efficiently buying a worthless metric.
