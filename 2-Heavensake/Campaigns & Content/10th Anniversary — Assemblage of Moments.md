## The Theme: Assemblage of Moments

*"An Assemblage of Moments"* — The thematic concept for HEAVENSAKE's 10-year anniversary, celebrating how the brand has brought sake into versatile, modern luxury settings — from fashion shows and art galleries to Michelin-starred dining and nightclub takeovers.

**The Decade of Purity** — The 2026 global celebration of the brand's founding, focused on "Reviving an Icon" and featuring high-profile events in Monaco and Tokyo.

## Visual Symbols for the Anniversary

*(From [[Sake Glossary]] — Symbols & Aesthetics)*

**The Torii Gate** — Used in the brand's merchandising and 10-year anniversary assets. Represents the passage from the mundane, everyday reality into the elevated "Heaven" of the brand experience.

**The Daruma** — A traditional Japanese doll symbolizing perseverance. Used by HEAVENSAKE for its 10-year anniversary ritual, where the act of painting the doll's eyes represents celebrating past achievements and looking toward a new chapter.

## Messaging Framework

**Anniversary Positioning:** *"The Decade of Purity"* — 10 years of reviving the drink of the gods.

**Thematic Anchor:** *"An Assemblage of Moments"* — Each moment over the past decade (from fashion backstages to Michelin tables to Ibiza clubs) is a component in a larger composition. Like the Assemblage itself: each element elevated the whole.

**Psychological Levers:** Activate Heritage (primary) + Exclusivity (secondary) for the anniversary campaign. The 10-year mark is a credibility story, not a sales event. See [[Psychological Levers]] → Heritage and Exclusivity.

**Persona Priority:** VHNW Collector (Prestige Anchor), Craft Connoisseur (Cultural Authority), Social Host (Core Growth). See [[Buyer Personas]].
