# Partnerships & Collaborations

The complete map of HEAVENSAKE's cultural partnerships, organized by category. These relationships are not endorsements — they are moments where HEAVENSAKE's world and another creator's world intersect to create something that neither could produce alone. Every collaboration must carry "cultural heat."

## Collaboration Vetting Framework

Every potential collaboration must answer:

1. **Does the partner have cultural heat?** (Not just prestige — heat. The brand equivalent of a save, not a like.)
2. **Does the intersection feel authentic?** (Would HEAVENSAKE actually drink at this person's table?)
3. **Does it generate media-worthy content?** (Photography, video, a moment that travels beyond the room.)
4. **Does it avoid "papa aesthetics"?** (No wax seals, no dusty calligraphy, no souvenir-shop Japan.)
5. **Does it create a DTC conversion pathway?** (Event guests must have a path to purchase, club membership, or CRM capture.)
