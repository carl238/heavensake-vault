## Key Takeaways
The team focused on ensuring a cost-effective approach for the anniversary event while retaining creative vision. Under scrutiny: a €38,000 quote for a custom mirror table. Carl sought detailed technical specifications to evaluate materials and future usability. Cost optimization strategies included renting tableware to save 20%, with strong emphasis on floral decor (orchids). The unique Torii gate design was praised for originality but raised IP protection concerns. Guest comfort for water-adjacent dining and logistical planning were also discussed. The event serves as a portfolio-building opportunity for the partners.
