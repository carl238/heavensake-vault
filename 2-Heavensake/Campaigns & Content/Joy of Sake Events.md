## What Is Joy of Sake?

Joy of Sake is one of the most prominent annual sake events in the United States, bringing together brands, importers, sommeliers, and sake enthusiasts for a large-format tasting. It operates in New York (and historically in other cities). In HEAVENSAKE's context, it is treated as a **core trade calendar fixture** — a guaranteed opportunity for face time with the sake trade community.

## Strategic Context: Joy of Sake within the Trade Event Landscape

Joy of Sake is positioned differently from HEAVENSAKE's own hosted events:

| Event Type | Example | HEAVENSAKE's Role |
|---|---|---|
| Own-hosted trade events | Hotel Okura Tokyo, ICA Sake Dinner | Host / designer |
| Partner-funded events | AP x Kurosaki San, Sotheby's Abu Dhabi | Featured guest / co-partner |
| Industry calendar events | Joy of Sake, World Sake Day events | Participating brand |

All three formats serve the brand, but the **industry calendar events** like Joy of Sake are important because:
1. They guarantee exposure to the concentrated sake trade community
2. Competitors are present — an opportunity to observe the field
3. Low logistics burden relative to hosted events
4. Relationship touchpoints with existing accounts and prospects
