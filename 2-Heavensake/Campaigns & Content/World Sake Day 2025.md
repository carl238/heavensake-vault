## Key Learnings & Post-Event Outcomes

### Content quality concern
- **General consensus across the team:** videography quality at US Sake Day events looked **"dégueulasse"** (cheap). This triggered a broader content quality push.
- [[Carl]]: "We need US content with chefs, absolutely."
- Led directly to the pursuit of higher-production shoots like ICA Sake Dinner (December 5) and Gojo Pop-Up.

### Distribution signal
- John Pak's Southern Glaciers distributor event gave direct access to accounts and reps not previously reachable — proving trade events are worth the logistics investment.

### Consumer behavior data
- Pogos Dallas QR code ordering: proved consumers would purchase on the spot with small discount incentive (20% off via QR). Data captured for HubSpot.
- Retail demo at HEB Plano: Sake Baby and Label Azure are the mass consumer entry points in Texas.

### Competitor landscape at NY events
- Major competitors present at World Sake Day NY: **Iwa, Dassai, Kubota**
- This is the clearest window to observe what competitors are doing and how HEAVENSAKE is positioned against the field.

### Network effects
- The Ibiza content → Nas → NY sushi accounts chain demonstrates the disproportionate ROI of high-quality international content feeding back into US market development.
- [[Nes Rueda]]'s reconnection with Wayne from Joji at NY events shows relationship capital returns from consistent event presence.

## Connection to Ongoing Strategy

- **Sake Day now a platform:** The brand should "own" International Sake Day each year. The AP partnership established a template: luxury watch brand funds event → HEAVENSAKE gets event, content, and association at zero cost.
- **US trade event differentiation:** Very few sake brands host their own trade events. Brooklyn Kura, Iwa, Dassai, and Sayono do — Soto does not. HEAVENSAKE's trade event investments are a competitive differentiator.
- **Prestige retail release:** October 1 was set as the target retail release date for Prestige One alongside Sake Day — combining a product moment with a cultural moment.
