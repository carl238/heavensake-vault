## The Activation: Material Good x HEAVENSAKE

### What Happened
- [[Nes Rueda]] coordinated a collaboration with **Material Good** for their Art Basel Miami event.
- [[Carl]] approved it explicitly as a **zero-cost brand visibility opportunity** that aligned with HEAVENSAKE's luxury positioning.
- [[Zak Gross]] and [[Nes Rueda]] attended and hosted.
- Video content was shot and **reshared** by the team.

### Why It Matters
Material Good is a luxury brand/retailer operating in the space where art, design, and high-end lifestyle intersect — exactly the brand adjacency HEAVENSAKE is building toward. Being present at their Art Basel event without financial exposure was a textbook example of HEAVENSAKE's preferred activation model: **high-brand-value, low-cost, invitation-based partnerships**.

### "Opportunistic" Framing (2026 Planning)
At the March 2026 meeting, when discussing Miami events for 2026, Art Basel was described as **"hard to activate but team should take any opportunities that arise."**
- This reflects a strategic stance: rather than buying expensive official sponsorships, HEAVENSAKE monitors for no-cost or low-cost invitations from aligned partners — as Material Good proved in 2025.
- The brand's luxury positioning means invitation-based activations are achievable; paying for association is rarely necessary.

## Lessons & Implications

### For Future Art Basel Activations
1. **Build relationships with brands in the Art Basel orbit year-round** — Material Good connection was available because of prior relationship development.
2. **Video resharing is table stakes** — every event should produce content that can be distributed. The Art Basel content was reshared promptly.
3. **The ICA + Art Basel December double demonstrates** that HEAVENSAKE can sustain multiple simultaneous brand moments if planning is coordinated.

### For Hong Kong Art Basel (China Market)
- China market in development (as of mid-2025); March timing of HK Art Basel aligns with "March Art Basel good time to visit China" observation from [[Carl]].
- Future potential: activate around HK Art Basel as the China market develops; 6 months needed for China to be ready.
