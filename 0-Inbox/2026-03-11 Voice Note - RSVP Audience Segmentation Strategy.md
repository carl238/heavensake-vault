# Voice Note — RSVP Audience Segmentation Strategy — 2026-03-11

## Summary
The team discussed the importance of segmenting audiences for optimizing campaign effectiveness, identifying three key groups: general brand awareness viewers, aspirational attendees, and affluent travelers. The need for precise geographic targeting, particularly in regions like Miami, New York, and Dubai, was highlighted, acknowledging the challenges posed by lacking data on spending power and travel likelihood. The group emphasized the significance of building retargeting infrastructure now for the upcoming Monaco event and leveraging insights for the Miami party in October. Despite recognizing the challenges in attracting wealthy U.S.-based travelers, there was a debate about focusing on European frequent travelers for better accessibility and potential attendance.
