## Summary

This is Carl's clearest articulation of his AI transformation thesis for HEAVENSAKE. He has been experimenting personally and intensively — reprogramming the website, cleaning operations, running restaurant prospect research — and he is genuinely mind-blown by what Perplexity Computer can do. The excitement is not abstract; he is describing live tests he ran.

The core strategic insight: HEAVENSAKE has $250K+ of commercial value sitting dormant in its existing database. The priority is not ads or new leads — it is building the operational and AI infrastructure to activate, segment, and nurture these existing contacts with hyper-personalized outreach at scale. Human labor for personalized email outreach doesn't scale; AI does.

Abdel provides the complementary technical view: the Notion operations infrastructure is now solid enough to build sophisticated automations on top of it. But the brand knowledge layer is missing — a gap that limits what AI can do autonomously for marketing and brand decisions. The implication: HEAVENSAKE needs to systematically encode brand knowledge (voice, strategy, DNA) into Notion so AI tools become full context-aware collaborators.
