## Summary
The team discussed the launch strategy for a limited edition pink Hanami bottle exclusively on Japan's e-commerce platform, capitalizing on the Hanami season with a price point of $500 for 50 to 100 bottles. Carl Hirschmann highlighted the importance of targeting women, a previously untapped demographic, and leveraging early sales signals to validate demand. Sebastien L’Hermitte is tasked with logistics coordination, ensuring readiness for payment, shipping, and packaging discussions with partners. The launch aims to build a customer base from scratch, with an emphasis on influencer engagement and local media coverage. The timeframe is set from March 28 to April 20, prioritizing a strong digital presence to enhance brand perception and drive consumer demand.

## Key Strategic Insights

- eCommerce launch mechanics: website, campaign, and channel activation plan

- Timing and campaign coordination for spring 2026 launch

## Carl's Thinking
The Pink Bottle represents Carl's ability to create product-moment alignment — launching a visually distinct product timed to a cultural moment (Hanami/cherry blossom season) is a classic luxury brand tactic for generating urgency and cultural relevance. The eCommerce focus also signals a direct-to-consumer push that captures the full margin on a high-visibility launch.
