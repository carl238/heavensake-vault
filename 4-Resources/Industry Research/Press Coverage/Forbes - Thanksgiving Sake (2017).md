**Publication:** Forbes

## Article Summary

An early Forbes mention of HEAVENSAKE, included in a broader article promoting sake for Thanksgiving. This is one of the brand's earliest major US press hits, featuring a quote from Nes Rueda, then Managing Director of HEAVENSAKE. The article advocates for sake's versatility as a food-pairing beverage and positions HEAVENSAKE as a go-to premium brand.

---

## Key Brand Description by Forbes

> *"The premium sake brand Heavensake"*

---

## Key Quote

### Nes Rueda, Managing Director, HEAVENSAKE

> *"Sake is incredibly versatile and intricate... Last year, I introduced sake at Thanksgiving, and it was thrilling to see how astonished and delighted my guests were by the range of sakes alongside the diverse seasonal dishes and sides."*

---

## Context & Significance

This 2017 Forbes mention is notable as one of HEAVENSAKE's earliest appearances in major US financial/lifestyle media. The positioning is clear: HEAVENSAKE is presented as a **premium brand** with a knowledgeable managing director who can speak as an expert voice on sake's food-pairing potential. The brand is used here to lend credibility to sake in general — Forbes trusted HEAVENSAKE's MD to be a spokesperson for the entire category.

---

## Brand Perception Analysis

Early Forbes coverage frames HEAVENSAKE as an authority in the premium sake space, capable of educating a mainstream American audience. The brand's managing director is treated as an expert, not just a marketer — an early sign of the brand's ability to generate media credibility beyond pure product promotion.
