## Tasting Notes (Wine Access)

> *"A razor-sharp translucent platinum decorates the glass. Aromas are led by blueberry fields cascading through a handful of jasmine blossoms and spice. The mouthfeel is elegant and elevated by layers of crushed-limestone minerality embraced by lemon curd, tension, and a cleansing acidity."*

> *"The sake has a bright, clear, platinum hue. Exotic notes emanate from the glass, moving seamlessly from wild, night-blooming jasmine and mandarin blossom to spices like clove and star anise, before ripe, wild-berry scents emerge. Luxurious yet sculpted on the palate, HEAVENSAKE drinks like a dream, progressing from fresh floral and fruit tones to a cleansing minerality that evokes images of Japan's mountain streams. Its laser-like precision is balanced with indulgent flavors, making finishing the bottle all too easy."*
