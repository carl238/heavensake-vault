## Brand Perception Analysis

Forbes' framing of HEAVENSAKE in October 2024 is clear: this is a **luxury brand making industry waves** through French winemaking technique applied to sake. The descriptor "making waves" positions the brand as disruptive within its category — a brand actively reshaping how the sake industry operates, not merely occupying a niche.

The publication chose to frame the story around the *technique* (assemblage), not just the founders, suggesting Forbes recognizes the methodological innovation as the brand's most compelling differentiator. The "cultural exchange between Japan and France" framing echoes the brand's own language, indicating strong message alignment in press coverage.
