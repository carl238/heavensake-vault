# HEAVENSAKE VAULT — How This Works

Welcome to the Heavensake Knowledge Vault. This is the single source of truth for everything that makes Heavensake what it is — and everything that makes Carl who he is.

---

## The Philosophy

Carl and Heavensake share DNA. The founder's thinking shapes the brand; the brand sharpens the founder's thinking. This vault keeps them **connected but separable** — linked through Obsidian's graph, but organized so each can stand alone.

---

## Folder Structure

| Folder | What Goes Here | Examples |
|--------|---------------|----------|
| **0-Inbox** | Everything lands here first. Process during weekly review. | Fireflies transcripts, raw notes, screenshots, ideas |
| **1-Carl** | Your personal thinking, philosophy, decisions, relationships | Principles, journal entries, decision rationale, key people |
| **2-Heavensake** | The brand as its own entity — DNA, voice, products, strategy | Brand Bible, Voice Guide, Never Say List, product notes, campaigns |
| **3-Operations** | Running the business — finance, distribution, tech, team | Invoices, distributor notes, infrastructure docs, team contacts |
| **4-Resources** | Reference material that doesn't change often | Industry research, competitor analysis, inspiration, templates |
| **5-Archive** | Completed projects, past campaigns, old versions | Anything no longer active but worth keeping |

---

## The Rules

1. **Everything enters through Inbox.** Don't file directly — dump it in `0-Inbox`, then sort during review.
2. **Link aggressively.** When a Carl thought connects to a Heavensake decision, link them with `[[double brackets]]`. This is the vault's superpower.
3. **Use templates.** Every note type has a template in `4-Resources/Templates`. Use them.
4. **Tag for cross-cutting themes.** Tags cut across folders:
   - `#carl-origin` — ideas that originated from Carl's thinking
   - `#founder-voice` — content written in Carl's authentic voice
   - `#brand-dna` — anything that defines what Heavensake IS
   - `#never-say` — language guardrails
   - `#campaign/sakura` `#campaign/10yr` — campaign-specific
   - `#market/us` `#market/japan` `#market/china` — market-specific
   - `#product/label-noir` `#product/prestige-one` etc.
5. **Properties first, tags second.** Use YAML frontmatter for structured data (type, status, product, date). Use tags for themes and connections.

---

## Weekly Review Ritual (15 min)

1. Open `0-Inbox` — process every note (move, tag, link, or delete)
2. Check `1-Carl/Journal` — write a quick reflection if something's on your mind
3. Scan `2-Heavensake/Brand DNA & Voice` — anything to update or refine?
4. Look at the **Graph View** — any orphan notes that should be connected?
