## Vault Principles

1. **One topic = one definitive note.** "DTC Strategy" is one note, not 15 meeting transcripts.
2. **Knowledge notes are written as facts**, not as meeting summaries. "HEAVENSAKE's DTC strategy is..." not "In this meeting they discussed..."
3. **Quotes are real.** Carl's direct quotes in synthesized notes come from the source transcripts.
4. **The archive is for searching, not for reading.** The synthesized notes are what you read every day.
5. **Do not include anything about SIA/SEYA.**
