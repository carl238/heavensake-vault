## Vault Rules

### 1. Status Tags (mandatory on all knowledge notes)

Every synthesized note must have one of these in its frontmatter:

| Tag | Meaning |
|-----|---------|
| `status: validated` | Confirmed by Carl or primary source. Treat as fact. |
| `status: needs-review` | Content exists but may be outdated or incomplete. Verify before acting. |
| `status: active` | Live SOP or ongoing process — consult before acting. |
| `status: merged` | This note's content has moved elsewhere. Follow the redirect. |

### 2. Wikilinks Only — No External Links in Notes

All connections between notes must use `[[wikilinks]]`. No Notion links, no Google Drive links, no external URLs embedded in knowledge notes. External references belong in the Archive.

### 3. One Topic = One Definitive Note

There is one note on DTC Strategy, one on Brand Voice, one on Team Structure. If you think a topic needs updating, edit the existing note — do not create a parallel note. Duplicates go to `5-Archive/Duplicates/`.

### 4. Knowledge Notes Are Written as Facts

"HEAVENSAKE's DTC strategy is..." — not "In this meeting they discussed..." Meeting language belongs in the Archive.

### 5. Quotes Are Real

Carl's direct quotes in synthesized notes come from the source transcripts in `5-Archive/Meeting Transcripts/`. They are not paraphrased.
