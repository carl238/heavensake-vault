# Tsushima San

**Role:** Master Brewer / President of Shichi Ken Brewery
**Company:** Shichi Ken Brewery (Japan)
**Relationship:** Key brewery partner for Label Azure and the proposed 10th anniversary Magnum Junmai Daiginjo; Carl's most trusted and admired figure in the sake industry after 12 years
**Contact:** Through Carl Hirschmann directly

## Context from Marketing Meetings

- **2025-09-22:** Shichi Ken won IWC award. Carl proposed storytelling arc for 10th anniversary Magnum: "Jumai Daiginjo Shichi Ken 10 Year Anniversary Blend in line with their IWC title and the friendship with Tsushima San, a great advocate of the future of sake. In my 12 years in the sake industry, he is the most sharp, reactive, efficient, supportive. Like he's an absolute monster. I love him."

## Notes

Tsushima San (president of Shichi Ken Brewery) is described by [[Carl Hirschmann]] as the single most impressive person he has met in 12 years in the sake industry — "the most sharp, reactive, efficient, supportive." This is a deeply personal endorsement from Carl.

Shichi Ken is the brewery behind Heavensake's [[Label Azure]] product, which Carl has also described as "my favorite at the moment" (Nov 2025). The 10th anniversary Magnum Junmai Daiginjo concept is centered on this partnership.
