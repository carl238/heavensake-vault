## Notes

Kurosaki San is Carl's most important Japanese chef relationship and arguably the most brand-aligned ambassador Heavensake has cultivated. He was flown to Milan and Ibiza for a September 2025 activation — the entire trip was funded by Audemars Piguet. He brought [[Kobayashi San]] (a third-generation sushi master from Niigata).

He has a prior relationship with [[Chef Nas]] (Sushi Nas, NY Michelin) — reportedly flew from Japan to New York just to cook with him, which was how Holly made the connection.

Carl has described him as: "young, good looking, cool, amazing chef. Totally aligned with our values." He is a key component of the 10-year anniversary concept.
