## Notes

David Grutman controls one of Miami's most influential luxury hospitality empires (Groot Hospitality: Komodo, LIV, Story, etc.). He has a personal financial stake in Soto sake, which is Heavensake's primary Miami competitor. This makes formal placement in his venues impossible for the foreseeable future.

However, Carl and [[Zak Gross]] view him as a valuable relationship to maintain — [[Nes Rueda]] has been inviting him to the Monaco Grand Prix party annually. If his Soto stake were ever to change, or if he opens new ventures (he is reportedly planning an Erewhon-type retail concept), there could be an opportunity.

His network connection to other Miami operators is also valuable even without direct placement.
