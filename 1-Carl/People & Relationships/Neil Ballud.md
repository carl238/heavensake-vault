## Context from Marketing Meetings

## Notes

Neil Ballud is a proposal still in very early stages — Nes Rueda surfaced him as a chef brand ambassador candidate in March 2026. His dual French-American identity makes him potentially well-suited to Heavensake's European brand with US ambitions. His current role at Four Seasons Hong Kong also creates an interesting Asia-Pacific angle.

The "expensive" caveat is worth noting — [[Carl Hirschmann]]'s preference has been for chefs who participate because they genuinely love the brand rather than for large fees (as demonstrated with [[Kurosaki San]] and the AP-funded activations).
