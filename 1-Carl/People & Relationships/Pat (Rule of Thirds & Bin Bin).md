# Pat (Rule of Thirds / Bin Bin)

**Role:** Manager at Rule of Thirds Restaurant / Bin Bin Sake Retail Shop
**Company:** Rule of Thirds / Bin Bin (New York)
**Relationship:** Controls one of only three dedicated sake retail shops in NYC; previously had a barrier to listing (required Bin Bin distribution); now open to events with Heavensake; [[Holly Reinsch]] is primary contact
**Contact:** Through Holly Reinsch

## Context from Marketing Meetings

- **2025-10-06:** Holly spoke with Pat; he wants to do an event. Rule of Thirds has associated sake shop Bin Bin. They also control the sake listing at a nightclub called "Heaven" (previously Heavensake couldn't get listed there because it required Bin Bin distribution).

## Notes

Rule of Thirds is a highly respected Japanese restaurant in NYC; Bin Bin is its associated sake retail concept — one of only three dedicated sake shops in New York. The fact that Pat is now open to events represents a meaningful opening in the NY sake retail circuit.

The irony noted by [[Carl Hirschmann]]: they couldn't get listed at the nightclub called "Heaven" because it required Bin Bin distribution — which they now have a path to.
