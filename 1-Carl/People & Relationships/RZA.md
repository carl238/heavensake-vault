**Role:** Hip-Hop Artist / Cultural Icon / Organic Brand Enthusiast
**Company:** Wu-Tang Clan
**Relationship:** Organic brand advocate — appeared on Drink Champs (4-hour podcast, 170K Instagram views) promoting Heavensake without payment; wants "all the Heaven Sake bottles there" and offered 20 minutes of brand talk for free; Carl exploring him as "deal-maker" rather than paid ambassador
**Contact:** Through Carl Hirschmann's network

## Context from Marketing Meetings

- **2026-06-23 (note: this likely refers to an earlier 2025 date):** Referenced in influencer strategy discussion.
- **2025-06-16:** Drink Champs episode went live. Carl: "~20 min of Heavensake promotion in a 4-hour podcast." ~170K Instagram views generated. Limited measurable traffic spike or DM increase initially. Carl wants a highlight reel edit from the footage. Exploring RZA as deal-maker (using his network) rather than traditional ambassador.
- **2025-06-16 (quote):** "RZA wants not one, not two — I want all the heaven sake bottles there. And I'm going to talk for 20 minutes about your brand. And I don't care if you're going to pay me or not."

## Notes

RZA's organic love for Heavensake is one of the most remarkable unsolicited endorsements the brand has received. He promoted the brand extensively on a 4-hour Drink Champs episode without any payment. Carl called the Prestige bottle "Cocaine White" (RZA's words) during the episode.

The challenge: 170K views but limited measurable conversion to traffic or sales — suggesting his audience, while large, may not perfectly overlap with Heavensake's target consumer (sake buyers at $200–$850 price points).

Carl's current thinking: explore RZA as a "deal-maker" — using his network connections to open doors with other celebrities or luxury brand partnerships, rather than as a direct sales ambassador.
