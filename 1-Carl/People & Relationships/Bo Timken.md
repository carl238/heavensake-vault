# Bo Timken

**Role:** Owner
**Company:** True Sake (San Francisco)
**Relationship:** Long-standing retail partner; industry OG in the US sake world; Carl has "a lot of respect and admiration" for Bo; True Sake sold out Heavensake Prestige allocation to top customers immediately via newsletter
**Contact:** Through Zak Gross / direct Carl relationship

## Context from Marketing Meetings

- **2025-09-22:** Carl referenced True Sake's 20th anniversary as an occasion to give them 20 Prestige bottles. "If you ask Bo Timken who are the most influential people in the sake space right now, he would know." Carl used him as the reference example for identifying niche sake OGs who can guide influencer strategy.
- **2025-06-23:** True Sake accepted $850 Prestige pricing; Bo sold to his top customers, sold out immediately via newsletter.

## Notes

Bo Timken founded True Sake — one of the only dedicated sake retail shops in the US and arguably the most influential in terms of building sake culture in America. His endorsement and sell-through velocity of the Prestige allocation (at $850, sold out immediately) is a strong proof point for the product's positioning.

More strategically, Carl views Bo as the type of "OG" connector who knows the true influencers in the US sake world — people who can help identify which micro-influencers, writers, and sommeliers actually matter. He is the archetype for the "people of influence" strategy.
