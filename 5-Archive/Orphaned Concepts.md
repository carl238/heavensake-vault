# Orphaned Concepts

Ideas that were explored, developed, or piloted but have been abandoned, paused, or placed on indefinite hold. These are not failures — they are proof-of-concept experiments that taught the brand something. Archived here so the institutional memory is not lost.

---

## 1. MAMIMOZI Valentine's Calligraphy Scrolls — SCRAPPED

**The Concept:** A luxury calligraphy scroll product featuring artwork by [[MAMIMOZI]] / [[Mami-san]], sold at ~$1,000 USD per scroll as a Valentine's Day / gifting item.

**Three tiers were developed:**
| Tier | Material | Price Target |
|------|---------|-------------|
| [[Harajuku]] | Nylon / Polyester (cheapest) | Low |
| [[Shimonoseki]] | Mid-quality materials | Mid |
| [[Kyoto]] | Premium materials | ~$1,000 |

**Why it was scrapped:**
- "You're going to sell one or two scrolls... The amount of headaches... the landing page just it's just not worth the money. The entire story is not worth the money." — Carl
- Operational drag was disproportionate to revenue potential
- Landing page cost, logistics, and customer service requirements made the economics untenable

**What was preserved:**
- The MAMIMOZI collaboration continues — for art installations, brand events, and content
- The scroll concept's artistic essence is alive; the commercial product model is dead

**Lesson:** Artist collaborations must be structured as cultural events or brand content, not physical product sales unless margin and operational infrastructure is pre-validated.

## 2. Recycled Bottle Carafes — PAUSED

**The Concept:** Cut used HEAVENSAKE bottles just above the "Heavens" text and shape them into carafes. Positioned as a 10-Year Anniversary concept combining sustainability, craft, and brand heritage.

**Why it's paused:** Not confirmed as killed — but no operational momentum as of March 2026. The anniversary merch focused on the Daruma doll and Torii Gate T-shirts.

**Potential revival:** Strong concept for DTC brand storytelling (sustainability + heritage + circularity). Could be positioned as a Club Heaven member benefit or event gift.

## 4. Apple Wallet "Sake Wallet Passes" — SHELVED

**The Concept:** Digital passes via Apple Wallet used as event access credentials / brand engagement gimmick for parties.

**Why it was shelved:** Questioned for actual ROI — the tech novelty did not translate into meaningful customer behavior change. Convenience benefit was minimal vs. standard QR code event entry.

**Potential:** Could be revisited if Club Heaven membership gains critical mass (membership card in Apple Wallet has permanence value).

## 5. Wooden Light Boxes — PAUSED

**The Concept:** Custom wooden light boxes for bar/restaurant displays — illuminated bottle displays to increase brand visibility at HoReCa accounts.

**Status:** The team was instructed to research manufacturers (potentially in China). No confirmed supplier or timeline as of March 2026.

**Rationale for revival:** Premium on-shelf presence is a genuine HoReCa pain point. A light box that makes the HEAVENSAKE bottle the most visible object on a bar would be valuable at Tier 1 accounts.

## How to Revive an Orphaned Concept

Before reviving any paused concept, answer:
1. Has the operational infrastructure improved to support this?
2. Is the margin math viable now?
3. Is there a DTC conversion pathway?
4. Does it generate content as a byproduct?
5. Does it belong in the HEAVENSAKE universe ("Stanley Kubrick meets Daft Punk")?
