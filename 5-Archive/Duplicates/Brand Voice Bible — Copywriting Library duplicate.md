# THE HEAVENSAKE BRAND VOICE BIBLE

*Source: BRAND-VOICE-10.pdf — Complete brand voice reference document*

---

## I. CORE IDENTITY & MISSION

HEAVENSAKE is a premium sake brand born from the cultural and technical fusion of Japanese craftsmanship and French artistry.

- **The Mission ("Revival, Not Definition"):** We are "Reviving" an icon. We are saving the "drink of the gods" that has fallen from grace with the younger generation. We never use the word "redefining" (which is generic marketing fluff used by competitors).
- **The Brand Essence:** A modern Franco-Japanese sake brand pioneering Assemblage — blending the purity of Japan's finest Junmai sake with the artistry and joie de vivre of French Champagne-making.

---

## II. BRAND PERSONALITY & TONE

The HEAVENSAKE voice is sophisticated yet approachable, blending the elegance of premium sake with a playful and inviting demeanor. It communicates a sense of luxury while remaining relatable.

### The Four Personality Traits

1. **Desirable:** A luxury lifestyle brand that is aspirational yet approachable.
2. **Unique:** The first to introduce Assemblage to high-end sake, setting a new standard of complexity and balance.
3. **Self-Defined:** Confident in its identity bridging Eastern tradition and Western elegance.
4. **Playful:** A sophisticated yet inviting tone, encouraging exploration.

### Tone & Style Rules

- **Cinematic & Sensory:** Open with strong imagery, evoke all senses. Use short, cinematic sentences for social media; use rich, immersive narrative for editorial and PR.
- **Wellness Luxury:** Highlight that the liquid is pure, additive-free, and aligns with modern wellness consciousness.
- **Avoid Jargon:** Avoid heavy sake jargon unless it is contextually explained.

---

## III. THE NON-NEGOTIABLE PILLARS ("THE TRUTHS")

- **Assemblage (Never Blending):** We do not "blend." Blending is for uniformity and cost-saving (mixing blue and yellow to get green). Assemblage is an artistic creation that elevates the liquid to a "third color" (like purple, born from blue and yellow).

- **The Duality of the Experience:**
  - *Pure Elevation:* Our public, elegant face. It represents cleanliness, luxury, and spiritual lift.
  - *A Better High:* Our visceral, lifestyle, and celebration engine. It represents the physiological benefit of the pure liquid.

- **The Ritual:** We drink from a wine glass, never an ochoko. This allows the floral nose to breathe and positions the brand on the white tablecloth.

## IV. THE HEAVENSAKE LEXICON (SEED PHRASES)

Use these approved hooks and anchor phrases to instantly immerse the reader.

### Sensory Hooks

- Silk on the tongue.
- A garden in bloom on the nose.
- Whisper of pear. Breath of jasmine.
- The sound of water meeting glass.
- First sip — a quiet rebellion.
- Moonlight in liquid form.
- The scent of spring through open shoji doors.

### Heritage, Craft & Nature

- Born in Japan, refined in France.
- Where Toji mastery meets French air.
- 20 centuries of brewing, reimagined.
- Eternity in a drop (Referencing the 15-year journey of our water through the mountains).
- An Assemblage of Moments (Expressing the versatility of the liquid across dinners, parties, art shows, and fashion).

### Signature Brand Lines

- HEAVENSAKE — The Art of Franco-Japanese Sake.
- Pure by nature, elevated by art.
- From Japan's finest breweries, blended by France's greatest cellar master.

## V. THE TARGET AUDIENCE (THE TRIBE)

- **Demographics:** Ages 28-50, upper-middle to high-income, well-educated, international travelers. They have a strong interest in cultural experiences and wellness-oriented lifestyles.
- **The "Cool Dad" & Creative Class:** Men who wear streetwear (Supreme, Neighborhood), drive vintage Porsches, and seek culturally relevant, unique experiences.
- **The Top 20% (The Whales):** The highly engaged luxury buyers who drive 80% of the revenue. They require VIP "Club Heaven" concierge treatment, rare allocations (like the $800 Pink Prestige), and personalized founders' notes.

## VI. CONTENT FORMATTING & EXECUTION

### Social Media (Instagram)
Treat the feed like a high-end magazine. Use a mix of visual styles. Captions must start with a powerful hook for the "scroller," but provide deep, rich context below the fold for the "reader."

### LinkedIn (Thought Leadership)
Use "Founders Content" (from Carl or Etienne). Discuss the struggle of saving a declining industry, the reality of building a global luxury brand, the "double niche" challenge, and the strategy behind winning the "Champions League" of business. Post 2-3 times a week, mixing deep-dive narratives with lifestyle.

### Campaign Specifics (e.g., The Sakura Prestige)
When launching limited editions, lean into powerful conceptual words like "Pink Resonance," "Rebirth," "Fleeting," and "Spring." Celebrate the craftsmanship and the women behind the bottle (the Toji, the Arita porcelain master).

## THE ULTIMATE FILTER

Before publishing, ask: **Is this generic? Is this something a standard agency would write?**

If it doesn't possess "Pure Elevation" or cultural heat, delete it and start over.
