## Key Strategic Insights
- **Sake education partnership** with Libby Sensei (sake sommelier)
- Developing sake education content and programs aligned with Heaven Sake's brand

## Carl's Thinking
Partnering with a sake somm like Libby reflects Carl's understanding that consumer education is the #1 barrier to sake adoption in Western markets — people don't buy what they don't understand. Using expert educators to demystify sake while associating that expertise with Heaven Sake is a smart brand positioning move that builds both category and brand simultaneously.
