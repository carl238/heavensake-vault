# Fractional CMO Search & Brand Scaling Strategy — 2025-08-06

## Summary
Heaven Sake, founded 12 years ago, has successfully transformed the perception of sake and aims to scale its operations in a competitive market. The company is led by GM Zach Rose, and Kristina Marino's experience in scaling beverage brands positions her as a strong candidate for CMO. Sake is marketed as a versatile and healthy alcohol, appealing particularly to men over 30 and health-conscious women in their late 20s, with peak consumption hours identified between 7pm and 1am. A strategic scaling plan is required, especially with a vacant New York sales position.
